Your time is precious. Use it wisely.

When you build a front-end application, you spend most of the time executing ordinary tasks. You create and validate forms, fetch data, show errors and confirmation messages, among other not really exciting things.

Those ordinary tasks, which very fastly become tedious tasks, sadly take too much time. And spending too much time on something that is not engaging does not result in delightful outputs. The hard truth is, while cars are going driverless, we programmers still forget to focus on the required field that is preventing the user from submitting a form.

Have you ever imagined if form controls had an attribute called validations that could validate themselves automatically on form submit? If success message was just one more attribute accepted by the form tag? Have you ever wondered not having to worry about showing or hiding a loader because the form tag itself is smart enough to take care of this? This is what a glorious library does for you.
